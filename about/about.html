Sortify / About

Welcome to Sortify

A browser-based playlist manager. Import, sort, and export your tracks and playlists, syncing with Spotify or using local files.

Getting Started

  1. Import your playlists Click "Import Playlists" on the dashboard. Choose Spotify (You'll be redirected to log in) or Local Files to load a file exported from here or another service.
  2. Open the Workspace Select one or more playlists from the Open Workspace card and click Open. All selected playlists appear as columns in a single table.
  3. Make changes and save Edits aren't permanent until you click Save. The Save button highlights when there are unsaved changes — save before navigating away.

How Sortify Uses Data

To review and edit your playlist library, Sortify stores your playlist data locally in your browser. It's completely private, and only leaves your device when you choose to export it.

Big downloads from Spotify can be slow, so a recommended approach is to export your whole library once, then save it locally as a JSON bundle for near-instant access next time you start a session.

Since data is saved in your browser between sessions, you can usually continue where you left off without needing to re-import, but downloading your data when finished ensures you have a backup.
